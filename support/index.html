Managing your subscription

Breath Premium is billed through your Apple ID. To view, change, or cancel it, open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Cancelling keeps your access until the end of the current period.

Bought Premium before and it isn't showing? Tap Restore purchases at the bottom of the paywall.

Apple Health

Breath can save each completed session to Apple Health as a mindful minute. It only ever writes; it never reads your Health data. Revoke access anytime in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

Reminders

Breath schedules reminders locally based on when you actually practise. Turn them off in Settings → Notifications → Breath.
