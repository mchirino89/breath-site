Breath — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Breath is a one-minute breathing app made by Mauricio Chirino. It is designed to know as little about you as possible.

What we collect

Nothing that identifies you. Breath has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics trackers of our own. Your breathing sessions, streaks, and preferences are stored only on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

Third-party services

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit processes subscription payments. We never see your payment details.
  • RevenueCat manages subscription status. It receives a pseudonymous app-generated identifier and your purchase receipt — never your name, email, or payment information. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.
  • Sentry receives crash reports (device model, OS version, and the technical state of the app at the moment of a crash) so we can fix bugs. Crash reports contain no personal data. See Sentry's privacy policy.
  • Google AdMob shows a small banner ad to free users only — subscribing to Breath Premium removes ads entirely. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads. We do not pass it any personal information ourselves. See Google's privacy policy and how Google uses ad data.

Apple Health

With your permission, Breath saves each completed session to Apple Health as a mindful minute. This is write-only: Breath never reads anything from Health, and your Health data stays on your device under Apple's control — we never see it, store it, or transmit it. You can revoke this permission anytime in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

Notifications

Streak reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data leaves your phone.

Data deletion

Deleting the app deletes all your data. There is nothing to request from us because we hold nothing.
